Do's and Don'ts on Social Media

Do's

1)Keep your profile courteous and professional.

2)Distribute uplifting and educational content

3)Utilise privacy settings to manage your information.

4)Check information before disseminating it.

5)Before publishing anything online, give it some thought.

Don'ts

1)Avoid disclosing private or sensitive information to the public.

2)Refrain from sharing inappropriate or offensive content.

3)Avoid disseminating unreliable or fake news.

4)Steer clear of negative remarks and cyberbullying.

5)Avoid using weak passwords or disregarding account security.
