**Preventive Checklist for Staying Safe Online**



1)Avoid clicking on unfamiliar or dubious links.

2)Prior to entering personal information, always confirm the URLs of websites.

3)Never divulge bank account information, passwords, or OTPs to third parties.

4)Make sure all of your passwords are strong and distinct.

5)Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA).

6)Steer clear of using public Wi-Fi to conduct financial transactions.

7)Update devices and apps frequently for security.

8)Watch out for messages that instill fear or a sense of urgency.## Financial Safety Advice for UPI

9)Verify UPI IDs twice before transferring funds.

10)Never accept unidentified payment requests or scan unknown QR codes.
